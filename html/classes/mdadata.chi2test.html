mdadata.chi2test

chi2test makes chi-square test for association

   res = chi2test(data);

The method makes the chi-square test for association between two qualitative variables (factors). It returns a table with chi-square statistic and the p-value for the test.

Examples

   load people
   people.factor('Sex', {'Male', 'Female'});
   people.factor('Region', {'A', 'B'});

   res = chi2test(people(1:25, {'Sex', 'Region'}));
   show(res)
