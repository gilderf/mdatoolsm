mdadata.crossresid

crossresid calculates standardized residuals for association of two factors.

   t = crossresid(data);

Standardized residuals shows an influence of one factor to distribution of proportions of the second factor.

Examples

   load people
   people.factor('Sex', {'Male', 'Female'});
   people.factor('Region', {'A', 'B'});

   r = crossresid(people(1:25, {'Sex', 'Region'}));
   show(r)
