mdadata.excluderows

exlcluderows exclude (hide) rows from the dataset

The method allows to hide rows without removing them from the dataset. The hidden rows will not be used in calculations, plots, etc.

Examples

   load people
   d = people(1:8, 1:4);

   disp(d)

   d.excluderows(2);
   disp(d)

   d.excluderows({'Lars', 'Lene'})
   disp(d)
