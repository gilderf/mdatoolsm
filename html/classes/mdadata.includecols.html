mdadata.includecols

includecols include (unhide) hidden columns

The method allows to unhide the excluded rows. Be aware, that the hidden rows have specific numeration, use showexcludedcols(data) to see the names and numbers of the hidden rows.

Examples

   load people
   d = people(1:5, 1:8);

   disp(d)

   d.excludecols(1:3);
   disp(d)

   d.includecols('Weight');
   disp(d)
