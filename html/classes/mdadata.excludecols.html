mdadata.excludecols

exlcludecols exclude (hide) columns from the dataset

The method allows to hide columns without removing them from dataset. The hidden columns will not be counted in any operations, calculations, etc.

Examples

   load people
   d = people(1:5, 1:8);

   disp(d)

   d.excludecols(2);
   disp(d)

   d.excludecols({'Height', 'Shoesize'})
   disp(d)
