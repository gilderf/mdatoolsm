mdadata.crosstable

crosstable calculates contingency table for combination of two factors

   t = crosstable(data);

Contingency table shows observed frequencies of every possible combination of two factor levels. If data has more than two columns, only first two will be taken.

Examples

   load people
   people.factor('Sex', {'Male', 'Female'});
   people.factor('Region', {'A', 'B'});

   t = crosstable(people(1:25, {'Sex', 'Region'}));
   show(t)
