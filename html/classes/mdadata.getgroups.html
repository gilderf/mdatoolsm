mdadata.getgroups

groups returns a dataset with binary values for all possible combinations of specified factors

   g = getgroups(data, columns);

The method gives an idea how grouping works. It is used in other methods for calculations and plotting of groupped data.

Examples

   load people
   data = people(1:10, :)

   people.factor('Sex', {'Male', 'Female'});
   people.factor('Region', {'A', 'B'});

   g = getgroups(people, {'Sex', 'Region'});
   show(g)
