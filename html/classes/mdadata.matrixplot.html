mdadata.matrixplot

matrixplot makes a 3D plot for dataset values.

   matrixplot(data);

The method shows a matrix of values from the dataset as a 3D surface with color identication of the surfacce levels.

Examples

   load people

   figure
   matrixplot(people(1:8, {'Height', 'Weight', 'Beer', 'Wine'}))
